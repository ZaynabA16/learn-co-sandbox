Our experts are creative and eager to work with you. Archiving primary sources from the web is an increasingly important activity of the stewards of community memnory. Public libraries often fill this role, but have historically made up only a small fraction of the growing web archiving community. The Community Webs program seeks to bridge that gap and make Web Archiving more approachable to public libraries.
